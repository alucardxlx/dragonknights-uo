<BASEFONT COLOR=#006400>PERKS:<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000>

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Corpse Retrieval Wand<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - For a fee, your Corpse will be brought to your location.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Special Craft Menus<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - Beside having custome added items in the craft menus, there are options where you can do AUTO LOOP, MAKE COUNT, and Smelt Item for the tailor and blacksmith.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Auto Harvest<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - To save your fingers, we have implamented a system that will allow mining or lumberjacking till the resource is depleated. The same goes for Spinning and Looming for the tailor.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Housing<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - You can buy certian buildings that have been setup for you to purchace, which you can sell back and get the money back that you spent on buying the place.
OR
You can make your own, (Via the House Placement Tool), which each account has a limit of having 4 houses. Houses will decay 90days from last refresh.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Accounts<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - You can have 3 Accounts per IP. So if you, spouse and child would like to all play together. If you need more then 3 accounts please contact a GM to assist you.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Character Slots<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - You can have 5 different characters per account.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Skill Cap<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - Is set to 900.00 points per character.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Stat Cap<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - Is set to 225 points per character.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Stat/Skill Purchase Crystal<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - This item will allow you to purchase Stats and skills. You can purchase up to 90 points on a skill. It will not allow you to purchase past 90, the rest you will have to work on yourself.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Skill Gems / Resist Sewing Kits<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - If you have the correct level Skill, you can use these items, and add skill bonuses to your armor, weapons, cloths, and jewlery from the Skill Gems. Or Add Cold, Fire, Energy, and Poison Resists from using the Resist Sewing Kits.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Evolution Dragons<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - A dragons egg, which if you have the right Animal Taming and Animal Lore you can hatch it, and raise your dragon to be stronger then a normal tamed animal. As it Evos you will see it grow.

<BASEFONT COLOR=#0000FF>Special Storage Keys<BASEFONT COLOR=#000000> - Which help a great deal with holding a lot of different items, giving you easy access to the stored items anywhere in the world.
